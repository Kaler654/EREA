        Instructions for using the book

        • 1. By highlighting text you can see the translation of a word or phrase, and add the highlighted word to your Dictionary.

        • 2. On the top right you can see the annotations in a given page of the book, you can also add the book page to bookmarks, turn on background music, or open the book in full screen mode.

        • 3. On the left you can see the list of chapters of the book, as well as your bookmarks.
