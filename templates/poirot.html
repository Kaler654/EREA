  • Poirot - an arrogant Belgian detective, he has an obsessive-compulsive disorder which means repeated unwanted thoughts or sensations (obsessions) or the urge to do something over and over again (compulsions). he finds it "really insupportable that every hen lays an egg of a different size! Poirot’s moustache was so important that Agatha Christie was asked to approve its appearance in the 1965 comedy mystery film The Alphabet Murders. Poirot thinks decaff coffee is an abomination.
